About us

The Warrington support group was set up by Yinka Dolan, July 2007, for people who stammer. The support group is based at the Halliwell Jones Stadium which is home to the Warrington Wolves Rugby Football team. The stadium is also home to the NHS and the Speech Therapy department, the support group is supported by Speech & Language Therapist Anne-Marie Jacob.

The support group can support each other in a safe and secure environment and more importantly help you to help yourself. Members discuss issues and concerns around stammering. This is a time where members can practise speaking any techniques they wish to use in a speaking circle environment.

Yinka Dolan has stammered since childhood but has greatly reduced her own fears of stammering, through research on the internet, various training courses, and via self help books, as a consequence, Yinka has reached a good deal of fluency. Yinka believes whole heartedly, that reducing fears associated with stammering and making changes to how you look at stammering from your beliefs, intentions, and your perceptions is the key to improved fluency.

The aims of the group are to:
  • Support each other in a safe and secure environment, help you to help yourself, discuss issues and concerns around stammering.
  • Reduce the fears associated with stammering.
  • Offer advice, information, where to get more help and possible grant aided training courses.
  • Practise any speaking techniques using speaking circles and role play.
