The Warrington support group was set up by Yinka Dolan, July 2007, for people who stammer. The support group is based at the Halliwell Jones Stadium, which is home to the Warrington Wolves Rugby Football team.

The support group can support each other in a safe and secure environment and more importantly help you to help yourself. Members discuss issues and concerns around stammering. This is a time where members can practise speaking any techniques they wish to use in a speaking circle environment.
